Humble Beginnings

This website is a little side project i'm working on to help build my web development skills. My coworker got me this day-to-day calendar from Amazon as a small gift on Christmas, and on the fourth day I had the idea to put it on tiktok. I had just started teaching myself HTML/CSS and needed a project to work on when my videos gained more views and I thought it'd be good practice to make a website with links to things on my desk and other little animal crossing features. I (of course) do play animal crossing and other video games, and I unfortunately also have a job so I can't update this as much as i'd like to, but i'd like to make this a functioning website that I can add to a portfolio by the end of the year. Don't be surprised if you see a ton of weird stuff going on with the pages and features lol. If you've gone this far then I appreciate you being curious enough to explore the site!
